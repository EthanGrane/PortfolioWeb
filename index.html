he Outsider is a short story created by Howard Phillips Lovecraft which conveys the loneliness and despair of human beings, my goal is to convey all those feelings of the story in a game between 1h to 2h and a half.

It is a first person game where you have to solve immersive puzzles in the Amnesia: The Dark Descent style, while you advance through the game a gloomy narrator as if it were a voiceover explains the thoughts of the protagonist of the story.

It all begins in a gloomy castle with high ceilings and long dark corridors where solitude and calm reign, the castle is infinitely horrible and there was never any light since some trees that surrounded the hateful castle did not let light in through the windows, until in an act of lucidity you decide to exit the castle to be able to see the light of day, even if it was for a moment.
